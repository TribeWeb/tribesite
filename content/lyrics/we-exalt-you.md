## Verse 1

Lord we stand to acknowledge Your greatness
Lift our hands to acknowledge Your grace
There is none like the Lord God Almighty
None can compare to Your glory and fame

## Verse 2

With a mighty hand You have led us
God our guide, our shepherd and king
Faithful God our rock and our fortress
It's to You we lift up our voice and sing

## Prechorus 1

Like Your name O God, let Your praises reach
To the ends of the whole earth
That all eyes may see and all ears will hear
Of Your magnificence and worth

## Prechorus 2

And with gratitude we raise a shout to You
Sovereign God we give You praise
We declare Your goodness and faithfulness
Your love endures and never fails

## Chorus 1

We exalt You
We exalt You
We exalt the name of the Lord x2

## Chorus 2

And we thank You
Yes, we thank You
Yes we thank the name of the Lord x2
