## Verse 1

When Your Word is spoken
By Your breath
We find identity
You call us as Your partners
Into maturity
So as You speak
Your mandate takes priority
Now we stretch our spirits
To their full capacity

## Chorus 1

You take our right hand
Subdue nations
Strip kings of armor
And open the doors
You go before us
Level the mountains
Break down the gates
Of bronze and iron
You give us treasure
Hidden in secret places
So we may know You are our Lord
Who calls us by name

## Bridge 1

So let Your house be built
On the foundation
Of our Global Civilisation
You spoke and now we see
It's our reality
