## Verse 1

My boundaries are set in pleasant places
Lord, you are my portion and my cup
And here I find my help and my salvation
I live beneath the shelter of your love

## Prechorus 1

All my life is found in you
All I am is yours
Alpha and Omega
You alone my source

## Chorus 1

Holy is the Lord
Holy is the Lord
