## Verse 1

God of Glory, God Almighty
We exalt Your name
All creation bows before You
And exalts Your name

## Bridge 1

Hear the cry of Your people calling
Heed the sound of Your holy ones
Look upon Your saints now ready
Respond to the shout of Your Bride and come!
Lord come!
