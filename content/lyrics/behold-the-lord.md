## Verse 1

Behold the Lord on His throne
King of Kings and Lord of lords
For His Kingdom is everlasting
His power and majesty endure

## Verse 2

Behold He reigns forevermore
Glorious One whom we adore
From age to age unchanging
Who was and is and is to come

## Verse 3

Behold the Lamb
Son of God
Conquering King and Lord of all
Sound Your trumpets, judge the nations
Let all know the Lord Almighty reigns

## Chorus 1

We magnify You
Place none before You
We glorify and bless Your holy name
Behold Your honor
Your saints together
Offering to You the highest praise
