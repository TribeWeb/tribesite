## Verse 1

You are our King
Here is Your honour
Our lifted hands
Our full surrender
Exultant praise is what we offer
With every breath
We bring our best before You Father

## Verse 2

You are our God
There is no rival
Beyond compare, Without an equal
Who was and is and is to come
The one eternal
Whose voice creates
Whose name is great among the nations

## Verse 3

You are our joy
Our deep desire
You stand alone
There's no one higher
So unto You Oh God our worship flows
From the rising of the sun until it goes

## Chorus 1

Great and awesome is Your name
And so it will remain forever and forever
x2
