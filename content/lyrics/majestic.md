## Verse 1

My eyes have seen You king
In Your glory and Your splendor
Woe is me, I am undone
Touched my lips and made me clean

## Verse 2

You purified me made me whole
Brought from darkness into light
For my eyes have seen you King
In Your majesty and might

## Prechorus 1

Holy, holy, holy
Are You Lord God Almighty
The earth is filled with Your glory

## Chorus 1

O Lord God
How majestic is Your name in all the earth
You are high and exalted
Lord Almighty, Lord of hosts
