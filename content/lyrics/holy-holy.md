## Verse 1

O Lord we stand in Your presence
A people formed by Your power and might
Lifting up our hands to the one who reigns
Forever and ever

## Verse 2

You have placed your seed within us
We will guard this treasure and keep it secure
Yes, the fear of You will be in us
Forever and ever

## Chorus 1

Holy, Holy, Holy is the Lord
Holy, Holy, Holy is the Lord
