## Verse 1

Here we are together
Descendants like the sand upon the sea
In Your image and Your likeness
And according to the promise
“So your seed shall be”
We praise Your name
Praise Your name together

## Verse 2

Reflecting on Your splendour
The glory and wonder of Your majesty
Every fibre of our being
Is arrested with a passion and spontaneously,
We praise Your name
Praise Your name together

## Verse 3

You created everything
For Your will and for Your pleasure
Sovereign King – Our God, Our Father
Let every tribe in every language
And in every tongue declare Your power
Your awesome works forever

## Verse 4

Hands lifted up high,
In honour of the one who holds eternity
With musical instruments skilfully
Voices raised in harmony
We praise Your name
Praise Your name together

## Chorus 1

When all has been said and all has been done
And we have arrived at what was to come
When one era ends and another begins
His praises we'll sing
Together, forever…
