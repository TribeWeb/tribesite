## Verse 1

Your greatness is unsearchable
Your tender mercies are over all Your works
We will speak of Your goodness to every generation
We declare Your glory in every land and tongue

## Verse 2

By Your grace we are lifted up
High above the darkness of the earth
By Your word You lead us
In You there is no fear
We come towards You boldly
We do Your will with joy
Joy

## Chorus 1

We rise to offer You our worship
We rise to glorify Your name
For You are great and greatly to be praised
So we bow before Your presence
And we rise to give You praise
