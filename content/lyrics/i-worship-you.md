## Verse 1

Only to You, I lift my hands
Only to You, I lift my voice
Only to You, I give my whole heart
Offer my life and worship You

## Verse 2

You reign O God, in strength and power
You are The Lord seated on Your throne
I see You move in sovereign authority
I stand in awe and worship You
