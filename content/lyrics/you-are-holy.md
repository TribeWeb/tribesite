## Verse 1

You are worthy of our praise, oh Lord,
We exalt Your holy name, oh Lord,
We reverence You alone, oh Lord.

## Verse 2

No-one else compares to you, oh Lord,
Sovereign, supreme, are You,
You reign in majesty and power.
