## Verse 1

Let my eyes see Your face
Let my heart hear Your voice
I stand in Your beauty, Your beauty
Let the earth fall away as You fill my gaze
I stand in Your stillness, Your stillness (Repeat)

## Chorus 1

Let Your Kingdom come
Let Your will be done
As we execute Your purpose here on earth
