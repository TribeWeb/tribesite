## Verse 1

I looked for a Man among them
That would build up the walls and
Stand in the gap on behalf of the land
A human component living in the same dark conditions
Closing the gap, impacting the land

## Verse 2

Creation groans
For the coming of the true sons
The earth awaits the coming of the true sons
Like Your word in Isaiah
We coming from a distant land
Here we are
Speak and let it be
