## Verse 1

I Lift My Hands
To the coming King,
To the great I AM,
To You I sing,
For You're the One
Who reigns within my heart.

## Chorus 1

And I will serve no foreign god,
Or any other treasure;
You are my heart's desire,
Spirit without measure.
Unto Your name
I will bring my sacrifice.
