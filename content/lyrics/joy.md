## Verse 1

Joy, joy as we praise
Joy as we lift a sound to You
Joy, joy in our hearts
Remembering all the things You do

## Verse 2

Here standing as one
We're bowing down to honour You
All thanks and all praise
Glory and strength are due to You

## Prechorus 1

With one voice and one heart
One mind and one sound
We exalt the name of the Lord!

## Prechorus 2

We exalt You
We exalt You
We exalt the name of the Lord

## Chorus 1

We magnify 
We glorify You
From everlasting to everlasting You reign
Lifting our hands
Lifting our voices
Alpha and Omega
We acknowledge and worship Your name
