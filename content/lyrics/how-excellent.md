## Verse 1

Oh Lord our God who's high above the heavens,
Whose reach extends throughout eternity,
By whom and for whom all things were created,
Who alone is lifted up in majesty.

## Verse 2

We will never cease to sing Your praises,
And to declare Your greatness day by day,
In the assembly of the just and holy,
Among the nations with one voice we say.

## Chorus 1

How excellent, how glorious,
Wonderful and marvellous, majestic,
Your name in all the earth.
Your sovereign power, we now declare,
Who will not bow, who will not fear, and worship
Your name in all the earth.
