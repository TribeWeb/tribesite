## Verse 1

You who speak and it's done
Declaring that which is yet to come
Existing by Your sovereign might
The path to truth, the path to life

## Verse 2

Eternity is in the palm of Your hands
Yet You dwell in the hearts of men
How awesome are Your works O God
How awesome Your ways

## Verse 3

When nations ask
How shall we describe
A God whose wisdom exceeds our minds
What shall we say, how shall we explain
You who transcend every spoken name

## Chorus 1

You Are Who You Are
(repeat)
