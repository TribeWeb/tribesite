## Verse 1

Worthy the Lamb that was slain to save us
Redeemed by His blood we are
Covered in Christ with our praise
We lift up holy hands to the Lord

## Verse 2

Praise to our God, our King is holy
The Lord God almighty reigns
Let us rejoice and be glad, give glory
Already aligned we say come

## Verse 3

Mighty the Lord our God who leads us
Unto the Finish we know
His glorious purpose revealed, victorious
With joy to His presence we go
