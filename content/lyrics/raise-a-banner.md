## Verse 1

We raised a banner on a bare hilltop
Lifted our voices and raised a shout
A great multitude marched on the mountain
In whom no compromise can be found

## Verse 2

The Lord of Hosts – has found Himself an army
From the ends of the earth we come
Tumultuous noise of the kingdoms of nations
Gathered together with a mighty sound

## Chorus 1

Now Church – Arise!
For our enemies are scattered
Oh saints arise!
For the princes have been driven away 
Shout it aloud!
And declare His kingdom is coming
Unleash the decree, the battle is won
On earth as in heaven
“Let Your will be done!”
