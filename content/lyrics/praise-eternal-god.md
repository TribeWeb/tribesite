## Verse 1

Praise eternal God of Nations
Time held in Your hand
Honour now the Lord's Anointed One 
Majestically enthroned.

## Verse 2

None can break Your Kingdom purpose
Princes try in vain.
You release Your grace empowering
Us to Christ proclaim!

## Verse 3

God You're sweeping through the nations
Calling sons to stand
“Come!” You call – a battle cry
Heralding the End

## Verse 4

Christ displayed in all His fullness
Wisdom manifold
Church arise mature, now meet your King
The Faith of saints fulfilled.

## Verse 5

Shouts of joy O God of Glory
Nations bring You praise
Your Word returns to You, our work complete
As we take our place.

## Chorus 1

Your throne O God will endure forever
In righteousness You reign
In majesty, ride victoriously
And bring glory to Your name
Who will not fear You O Lord our God
All will worship you alone
Honour now the Lord's Anointed One 
Majestically enthroned.
