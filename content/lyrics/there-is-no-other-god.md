## Verse 1

Your grace has brought us through
Presented unto to You
A people radiant and pure 
Steadfast in Your way
Willing to obey
Faithful and committed to Your purpose
Our hearts and minds You have aligned 
To Your pattern and design

## Chorus 1

Our lives they manifest You
Now all the earth can see
The glory of the one who holds all power
Your light now fills the nations
And all the world will know
That you alone are God 
There is no other God
