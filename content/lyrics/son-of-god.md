## Verse 1

All creation gives You glory
All Your people shout Your praise
Joining angels in the Heavens
Giving honour to Your name

## Verse 2

You are enthroned in the heavens
Seated high above the earth
Lord, You reign with sovereign power
And Your kingdom never ends

## Verse 3

Make a highway, now be ready
For He comes in majesty
All you nations call upon Him
Righteous Son of God is He

## Chorus 1

Hallelujah to the Risen One
In great power and sovereign strength He comes
Great Redeemer, now the Judge of all
Jesus Christ, the Son of God
