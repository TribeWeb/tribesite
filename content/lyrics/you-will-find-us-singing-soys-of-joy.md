## Verse 1

What a beautiful thing it is to keep watch for Your return
What a marvellous thing it is to see You coming for us
What an awesome thing to see you judging all the earth
What a beautiful thing it is to know we are Your bride

## Prechorus 1

When You come
When You return
When Your glory fills the earth

## Chorus 1

You will find us singing songs of joy,
You will find us lifting up Your name,
You will find us honouring Your ways,
You will find us giving You Your Praise

## Bridge 1

Holy, Mighty, Awesome God
We exalt Your Name
Holy, Mighty, Awesome God
We adore Your ways
