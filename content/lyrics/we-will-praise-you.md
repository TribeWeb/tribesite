## Verse 1

We join our hearts as we join in song 
More than just a melody 
We offer up the harmony 
Of the sound of lives 
Committed to You 
Awesome God, Wonderful 
What else could we do?

## Verse 2

Awaken me with the sound of Your voice 
I will listen I will listen 
Instruct my tongue O Sovereign Lord 
I will listen I will listen 
My eyes are lifted up to You! 
My hands are lifted in surrender 
My heart has been captivated 
As You have loved me
