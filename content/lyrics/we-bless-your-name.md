## Verse 1

There's none like you
We bring our praise to you
Our praises rise and glorify the Lord
Our hearts are turned to you
We honour You alone
There is no one higher than our God

## Verse 2

From strength to strength
Until we see your face
We'll walk with you our God eternally
Our eyes are fixed on You
We worship You alone
Almighty God, beginning and the end
