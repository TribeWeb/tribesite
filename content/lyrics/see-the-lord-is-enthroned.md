## Verse 1

You have made us Your people forever
You have chosen us, as Your own
Surely there is none, like the Lord
See the Lord is enthroned

## Verse 2

I know who I am, I belong to You
I came from You, I return to You
While I am here, You are my own
I live boldly before Your throne
