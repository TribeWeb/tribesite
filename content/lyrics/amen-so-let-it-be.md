## Verse 1

You are the God of all creation
All things are under Your command
Bring Your judgment
To the nations
Cause Your righteous ones to stand

Send Your word throughout the nations
Let Your chosen hear Your call
Make us one, Your holy people
Reveal Your glory now to all
