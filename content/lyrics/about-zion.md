## Verse 1

The day, the day of which we heard
Life within a broader place has begun
The hearts of the fathers have been turned
And the sons have learned, 
To whom honour belongs

## Prechorus 1

New wisdom we're receiving
Favour and grace to build
A nation which shines like the sun
All people flowing to it
And if you will receive it
This is that which was to come

## Chorus 1

Walk about Zion,
The city built with strength
All about Zion,
Arrayed in excellence
Talk about Zion,
The city of the Lord
So generations yet unborn
Our daughters and sons,
Can walk within its light
