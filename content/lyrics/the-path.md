## Verse 1

We regard the past no more
He is leading where no one has gone before
There are traces in the wind
We Perceive it!
The Lord does a New Thing

## Prechorus 1

In hearts that once lay bare
Abundance has appeared
And the pathway to fulfillment
Of His will is now made clear
A safe place has been found
New grace is flowing down
Strength and wisdom for completion
Of the mission of the Lord

## Prechorus 2

Send Your sound O God throughout the earth
Draw the chosen ones
And together we'll arise and shine
In You our light has come

## Chorus 1

Now darkness fills the land
And no one understands
We are surrounded by conditions
That shake the hearts of men

We stand among the wise
Among those who arise
Lead the nations to this pathway
And open up their eyes

## Chorus 2

We regard the past no more
He is leading where no one has gone before
And His presence will ensure
This path we take will be traversed by many more
