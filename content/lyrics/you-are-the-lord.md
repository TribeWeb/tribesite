## Verse 1

We walk in the ways of Your shepherds
We follow the path of Your saints
We sit in the seat of Your holy One
We delight in Your law and Your ways

We lift up our eyes to the One above
We fix our gaze on Your Son
We give all we have 'til Your will is done
And we enter Your presence with praise
