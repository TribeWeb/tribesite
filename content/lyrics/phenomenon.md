## Verse 1

You are high and lifted up
Your glory fills the earth
With one voice we lift You up
So let our sound be heard
Around the throne of Your dominion

## Verse 2

Let the worship of our hearts
Now rising through the skies
Break the barrier 
And split the heavens open wide
Let Faith arrive at full expression

## Verse 3

You are high and lifted up – Holy
To each other we declare Your glory
How great is the throne of Your dominion
We see You and a mighty sound initiates
Thick and towering is the cloud of worship we create
Our hearts overflow with adoration

## Chorus 1

Our ever, forever

Holy, Holy

Holy, Holy

## Chorus 2

How awesome – the measure, the stature

## Chorus 3

We laud You, exalt You

Holy, Holy

## Chorus 4

From every nation, tribe and tongue
