## Verse 1

We made the journey
This is the edge of time
You walk among us
Your people are aligned
With faith for the Finish
We offer victory
Together knocking with importunity

## Prechorus 1

Hear the cry of all our hearts
To cross the Border
Hear the sound of those who march
Like the army of the Lord

## Chorus 1

A thunderous host
Voices echo
Warriors shout in mighty victory
Advancing rampant
Lead us triumphant to immortality

The seed of wisdom
Remains secure with hearts of Your people
Awaiting Your decree
Commendation, transformation, immortality!

## Chorus 2

Amen! It is done!
The Finish come
For the One who sits upon the throne
The Faithful and True
Has made all things new
And to Him alone

Be strength and honour
With sovereign power
Our God has judged the nations
And brought us home to be
With Christ together, in Christ forever Immortality!

## Bridge 1

One body asking, seeking
All Your saints are knocking
Accelerating the day
The door finally opens
Mortality is broken
And Your mighty host will say
