## Verse 1

Sovereign God, Sovereign God,
We have come to honour you,
Alpha and Omega,
We bow down to worship you.

## Prechorus 1

Yours is the power,
Yours is the wisdom,
Yours is the honour and praise.

## Chorus 1

Glory to God in the highest,
Glory to God in the highest,
All glory and all strength we ascribe to you,
Lord majestic, Sovereign God we honour you.

## Bridge 1

All in your temple cry glory, glory,
We ascribe glory and strength to you,
Forever you will reign, glory, glory,
Sovereign God we honour you.
