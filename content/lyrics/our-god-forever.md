## Verse 1

Great are You Lord our God
And most worthy of praise
We lift our worship to You
Glorify Your name
Your presence surrounds Your people
We are safe and secure
Rejoicing in Your Judgements forevermore

## Verse 2

Great are You Lord our God
Let the earth shout Your praise
Let nations come to Your light
Walk in Your ways
You fill our hearts with goodness
We delight in Your truth
Rejoice in Your righteousness
And unfailing love
