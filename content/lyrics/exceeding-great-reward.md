## Verse 1

One thing have I desired,
I'll pursue with all my heart.
That I may dwell forever,
In the presence of the Lord.

## Verse 2

Bless the work of my hands
It will never shift my gaze
Place the nations under my command
My pursuit remains the same

## Prechorus 1

I was glad when they said,
Let us seek His face,
Let us learn of His laws,
Let us walk in His ways.
I rejoiced when You came close,
Engaging me,
Face to face and friend to friend,
And promised to be.

## Prechorus 2

I was glad when You said, 
“Come and seek My face,
Enter boldly into My Most Holy Place”
One thing have I desired,
This one thing I do,
I press for the excellence of the knowledge of You.

## Chorus 1

My Lord, my God, my exceeding great reward.
My Lord, my God, my exceeding great reward.
