The Bible is big! So anything that helps get the "map" into our minds is helpful for getting around.

The intention is to show:

- There are profound links between books through parallel stories, common images and characters.
- There is a broad but simple overarching structure that each book fits into.

A full screen version can be found at [/apps/bible-index](/apps/bible-index)
